Delicious Mango-Banana Smoothies

Description

Mango-banana smoothies are a great treat to have and easy to make. This smoothie is made up of mango, banana, and some added orange, giving it a slightly tangy yet floral and sweet flavor. Plus, this smmothie has lots of nutrients, making it a healthy snack.

Smmothies are a great treat to have to beat the summer heat. They're versatile as well, making it possible to add just about any fruit or vegetable you want. It's up to you to choose to make it into a healthy snack or delicious dessert, but either way, be sure to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 C frozen mango pieces
  • 1/3 C plain yogurt
  • 1/2 C orange-mango juice blend

Steps

  1. Combine the banana, mango, yogurt, and juice in a blender.
  2. Blend in blender until nearly smooth.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
