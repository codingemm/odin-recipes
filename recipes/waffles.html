Crispy Waffles

Description

Waffles are a delicious way to start off the new day. The ideal waffle has a nice, golden brown color to it and is crispy, light, and fluffy. They go well with butter, syrup, jam, fruit, and can be made into a variety of different flavors.

They don't just have to be a breakfast food. Waffles also are great for any meal of the day. You can make and serve them with some crispy chicken & syrup, or use them as the cone to your ice cream dessert. However you decide to use your waffles, you're sure to enjoy them.

Ingredients

  • 2 C all-purpose flour
  • 1 TSP salt
  • 4 TSPs baking powder
  • 2 TBSPs white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ C warm milk
  • ⅓ C butter, melted
  • 1 TSP vanilla extract

Steps

  1. 1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar, & set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. 2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs, then stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture, & beat until blended.
  3. 3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
